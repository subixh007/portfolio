I'm Subish — a creative developer and designer living at the intersection of art and technology. I believe great design is felt before it is seen.

My practice is built on intentional craft. Every decision, from typeface to transition, is deliberate. I work with brands and individuals who demand more than the ordinary.

"Design is not just what it looks like. Design is how it works."

When not designing, I am exploring new visual languages, studying architecture, and finding beauty in the overlooked.
